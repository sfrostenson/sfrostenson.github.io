#GivingTuesday Doubled Donations in Second Year


Raymund Flandez, Cody Switzer and I provided live coverage of #GivingTuesday 2013, which emerged in 2012 as a day of national giving. I worked with Network for Good to publish real-time results on online donations and secured donation totals from Blackbaud and PayPal to further inform our reporting.

In 2013, more than 10,000 nonprofit organizations participated as compared to only 2,500 nonprofits in 2012. Additionally, online donations doubled from $12-million to $21-million, according to data from Blackbaud and Network for Good, two organizations that process online gifts for nonprofits. PayPal said donations given through its site doubled from last year but didn't release figures.
