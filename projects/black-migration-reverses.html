Mapping the Reversal of the Great Migration


Using American Community Survey data from 2005-2010, I worked with Paul Overberg to visualize the movement of African Americans in and out of metros. I relied on D3 primarily for the front-end presenation and used Python to convert the spreadsheet data into a nested json file.
